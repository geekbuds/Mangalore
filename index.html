About us

We are bringing together Mangalore’s top influencers, creators, and digital entrepreneurs for the biggest and first-ever influencer meetup in the city! Our mission is to build a thriving creator community, foster collaborations, and provide opportunities for influencers to grow and expand their reach.

What is our goal

Our goal is to build a strong and vibrant influencer community in Mangalore, where creators, brands, and businesses can connect, collaborate, and grow together. We aim to empower digital creators by providing a platform for learning, networking, and unlocking new opportunities. This event is designed to celebrate Mangalore’s digital talent, foster meaningful partnerships, and open doors to exciting collaborations. By bringing influencers together, we strive to create a space where creativity thrives, ideas flourish, and success stories begin.
